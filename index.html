MR Kitchen Food Selis

Modern Street Food Concept with Electric Mobility

Business Overview

MR Kitchen Food Selis is an eco-friendly mobile FnB business leveraging electric bicycles to deliver efficient and flexible food services. The concept is scalable and supported by a franchise-based growth model.

Signature Product

MR Kitchen Food Selis delivers an elevated fried chicken experience, distinguished by a uniquely refined crispiness that sets it apart from unlike conventional fried chicken, paired with rich, layered flavors developed from a proprietary selection of premium spices. Every product is meticulously prepared to ensure superior taste and uncompromising quality. The offering is further refined with aromatic spiced soups delicately infused with selected herbs and spices, and complemented by an exclusive range of signature desserts—creating a distinctive, and memorable culinary experience.

Franchise Opportunity

MR Kitchen Food Selis offers a structured franchise opportunity designed for scalable growth and operational efficiency. This franchise model is ideal for investors and entrepreneurs seeking a flexible, mobile, and sustainable food business with strong market demand.
